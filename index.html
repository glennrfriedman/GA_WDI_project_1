SnakeBoy: Color

Instructions
Hello, welcome to SnakeBoy: Color.
/
The object of the game is to collect as much food as possible for SnakeBoy. SnakeBoy is the orange box that starts in the top left of your screen. Move SnakeBoy by using the arrow keeys (up, down, left right). Everytime SnakeBoy eats a piece of food another piece will be generated as well as a new obsticle. Avoid obsticles and the walls of SnakeBoy's box to keep playing!
